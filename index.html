Welcome to my personal site, here I share my stories from the outdoors. My favorite pursuits are fly fishing and upland bird hunting, but opportunities in the mountain west are nearly endless and I can't help but dabble in big game hunting, waterfowling, spin fishing, and more.

I've linked my youtube channel and substack blog, these sites are where I share my adventures from the field and post my long form written content. The Sportsman's Journal on this site is a photo-centric blog that I use to record my hunting and fishing trips for posterity. The Bookshelf is where you can find my written reviews of outdoor and outdoor adjacent media.

It doesn't matter if you're a well seasoned sportsman or if you're just curious about an outdoor lifestyle, you're welcome to read on and participate in the conversation by commenting or direct email. Thanks for visiting!
